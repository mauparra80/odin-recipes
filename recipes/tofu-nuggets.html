Fried Tofu Nuggets

Description

I literally cannot enjoy tofu any other way. This recipe takes your bland soft tofu and adds a crunch along with slight flavor. Seriously.. this tastes just like some good ol chicken nuggies

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of the most firm tofu you can find
  • panko bread crums (italian seasoning optional)
  • corn starch
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. 1 hour before cooking, start by draining the tofu, wrapping it with a few paper towels and pressing it under someting heavy. This is done to remove as much liquid from the tofu
  2. Once an hour has passed, prepare a skillet with 1/2 inch of vegetable oil and set to medium heat
  3. Cut the tofu block into 1 inch squares
  4. in a large mixing bowl, add 1 tbspn corn starch, 1 tblspn panko bread crums and lightly season herbs on hand
  5. toss the tofu cubes in the bowl and lightly mix until evenly covered
  6. Finally, fry the tofu being careful to not overcrown the pan. turn the tofu over after a few minutes until golden
  7. once golden on both sides, remove tofu and place in paper towl. add salt to taste and Doneski
