Creamy and Cheesy Spaghetti

Description

Make your own delicious creamy spaghetti following this easy recipe that is ready in just under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of noodle of your choice (linguini preffered)
  • 1 8oz tin of tomato sauce
  • 1 12oz jar of mexican table cream (any table cream will work)
  • 1/2 pound bag of mozzarella cheese
  • 1tblspn chicken broth powder

Steps

  1. Start by boiling water with a pinch of salt
  2. once water is lightly boiling, toss in the linguini and stir until cooked to desire
  3. drain but do not rinse
  4. in the same pot, set heat to low and heat up the tomato sauce (i think... or the cream first... I forget)
  5. once tomato sauce is warm, pour in the table cream and chicken broth
  6. Stir for a few minutes until mixed through and slowly add cheese while stirring.
  7. serve with protein of your choice and garlic bread. Yum!!!
